World's Best Lasagna

Lasagna is a difficult recipe to make but this recipe is customizable to suit your needs.

  • Meat: Either ground beef or Italian sausage, you can mix both.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion with two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add flavor.
  • Tomato products: A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add some subtle sweetness and enhance the sauce's flavor.
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: Helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't just ooze out of the lasagna when you try cutting into it.

How to make lasagna step-by-step

This is a brief overview of what you'll expect when you make lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
