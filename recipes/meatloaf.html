Classic Meatloaf

If you're in need of a delicious comfort food, then this recipe is for you!

  • Ground Beef: This calls for ground beef, but others prefer to use other types of ground meat.
  • Vegetables: Nearly-pureed carrot, celery, onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, and garlic will keep the meatloaf flavorful and moist.
  • Worcestershire Sauce: A tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce will give the meatloaf a good flavor.
  • Egg: Will helps the meatloaf together.
  • Spices: Italian herbs, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper to season the meatloaf.
  • Breadcrumbs: Will help bind the meatloaf together.
  • Glaze: The glaze is made with brown sugar, ketchup, Dijon mustard, and sriracha.

How to make meatloaf.

This is a brief overview on what you'll expect when you make meatloaf.

  1. Chop the vegetables: Put the vegetables and garlic in a food processor until nearly pureed.
  2. Form the meatloaf: Combine the vegetables, ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, spices, and the egg. Sprinkle in the breadcrumbs and mix with your hands (don't overmix).
  3. Bake: Form the meat mixture into a load then place in a prepared baking pan. Bake for about 15 minutes.
  4. Make the glaze: Combine brown sugar, ketchup, Dijon, and Sriracha to make the glaze.
  5. Glaze the meatloaf: Spoon the glaze over the slightly hot meatloaf. Return the meatloaf to the oven and bake until the internal temp reaches 160 degrees F.
